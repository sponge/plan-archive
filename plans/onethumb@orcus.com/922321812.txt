- 3/24/99 -

-[ TNT and 1600x1200? ]-


Ok, I'm dumb or something.  I have a TNT at home, and I've installed a
handful of them here at the office.  They all worked fine, allowing me to
go up to 1600x1200 in the Display Properties section.  In fact, they would
let me go to 1920x1200 if I had a widescreen monitor.

This board, however, will not let me.  It's a 16meg AGP Diamond Viper V550,
and apparently it only likes 1280x1024 in this machine.  I had it in another
machine running at 1600x1200 just fine though, so I'm a bit confused.

I've upgraded it's BIOS, tried the latest Diamond drivers, latest reference
drivers from Nvidia, and even tried some older drivers.

My monitor is set to the drivers that came on a disk with the monitor.  It's
a high-end EIZO FlexScan FX-E7 monitor, and it was running on this same card
in another machine at 1600x1200x32bpp @ 85hz just fine.  I also tried a
generic Plug & Play monitor setting, and a generic SVGA 1600x1200 setting.

No luck.

Anyone out there a total TNT guru?  I'm going to start digging around in
my registry to see what I can find, but I really miss my 1600x1200 desktop
and I'm getting frustrated.

Thanks in advance for any help.
